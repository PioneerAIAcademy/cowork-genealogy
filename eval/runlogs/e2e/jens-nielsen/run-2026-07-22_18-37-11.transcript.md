# Locality Guide: Lyngs Parish, Refs Herred, Thisted Amt, Denmark (1800–1870)

## Jurisdiction overview
- **Parish:** Lyngs (Lutheran)
- **Herred (district):** Refs
- **Amt (county) 1794–1970:** Thisted
- **Amt before 1794:** Vestervig
- **Diocese (Stift):** Viborg
- **Chapelry (Pastorat):** Thyholm Pastorat
- **Municipality:** Hvidbjerg-Lyngs
- **Administrative center:** Thisted (amt seat)
- **Population:** Reliable figures not returned by tool (place not found by population tool); small rural Lutheran parish on the Thyholm peninsula in northwestern Jutland
- **Economy:** Primarily agricultural; smallholding farmers (husmænd) and tenant farmers (fæstere)
- **Religion:** Lutheran (Church of Denmark) exclusively in this period
- **Key historical context:** Danish patronymic naming system in full use — children take surname from father's first name (Nielsen = son of Niels; Andersdatter = daughter of Anders). First child born to Jens in Lyngs in 1846; marriage likely c. 1844–1846.

## Boundary changes
- Pre-1794: Lyngs belonged to **Vestervig amt**; records from this period filed under that jurisdiction
- 1794–1970: Lyngs falls within **Thisted amt**, Refs herred — the operative jurisdiction for the 1840s–1850s target window
- FamilySearch standardPlace for target period: `Lyngs, Refs, Thisted, Denmark`
- Note: Boddum parish (Jens's birthplace) is also in Refs herred, Thisted — same administrative unit; its records are held and indexed in parallel
