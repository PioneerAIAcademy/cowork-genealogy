# flynn-competing-fathers

Patrick Flynn parentage research with two competing father candidates.

Extends mid-research-flynn by adding a second candidate father (Thomas
Flynn of Luzerne County) as hypothesis h_002. A death record for the
Luzerne County Thomas has been found showing he died in 1840 — five
years before Patrick's estimated 1845 birth — creating a chronological
impossibility that should lead to h_002 being ruled out.
