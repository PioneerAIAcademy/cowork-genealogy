## Notes for reviewers

Ground truth is the two children linked in the FamilySearch tree: Joleen
(b. 1 May 1946, Payson, Utah; d. 23 May 2020) and Patricia Ann (b. 21 Dec 1948,
Nephi; d. 3 Dec 1970), both deceased and both buried at Vine Bluff Cemetery,
Nephi. The starting tree retains Kenneth, his wife Ruth Tolley, and both of his
parents (John Henry Zabriske and Gertrude Marie Elnore Nielsen), along with all
of Kenneth's own attached sources (1950 census, marriage index, obituary, Find A
Grave / BillionGraves). Only the two children and the four ParentChild edges
linking them to Kenneth and Ruth were removed.

Note: Ruth Tolley's 2008 obituary also names later children (Terri Kay, Kenny)
who are not present in the stripped tree. If a run recovers those as well, they
are legitimate bonus finds, not errors — the two required findings are Joleen
and Patricia Ann.
